The City of New Dawn（全四级高频词全覆盖英文故事）

Liam was an ordinary college student who lived in a vibrant urban district of New Dawn City. Unlike the noisy downtown areas full of trivial chaos, his residential community was quiet and well-managed, with neat streets and green belts that softened the tense climate of the fast-paced modern city. Every morning, he would stroll along the sidewalk, watch the sunrise penetrate the thin mist, and adjust his mindset to embrace new daily challenges. He was not a supreme genius, nor a greedy person chasing endless wealth, but merely a young man who longed for a stable life and steady self-improvement.

【本段单词】：
1. urban /ˈɜːbən/ adj. 城市的
2. residential /ˌrezɪˈdenʃl/ adj. 居住的，住宅的
3. trivial /ˈtrɪviəl/ adj. 琐碎的，微不足道的
4. chaos /ˈkeɪɒs/ n. 混乱
5. climate /ˈklaɪmət/ n. 风气；气候
6. modern /ˈmɒdn/ adj. 现代的
7. penetrate /ˈpenətreɪt/ v. 穿透，渗入
8. tense /tens/ adj. 紧张的
9. supreme /suːˈpriːm/ adj. 最高的，极致的
10. greedy /ˈɡriːdi/ adj. 贪婪的；渴望的
11. merely /ˈmɪəli/ adv. 仅仅，只不过

【段落翻译】：利亚姆是一名普通的大学生，居住在新曦城一片充满活力的城区。不同于喧嚣杂乱的市中心，他所住的住宅小区环境安静、管理有序，整洁的街道与绿化带缓和了这座快节奏现代化城市紧绷的整体氛围。每天清晨，他都会沿着人行道漫步，看着朝阳穿透薄薄的晨雾，调整心态迎接全新的日常挑战。他并非天赋绝顶的天才，也不是贪求无尽财富的人，只是一个渴望安稳生活、稳步提升自我的年轻人。

Liam’s major was sociology, a discipline that enabled him to observe human behavior and social rules from a unique perspective. He held the standpoint that every social phenomenon had hidden underlying reasons, and no incident happened without a cause. In his spare time, he refused to idle away hours on meaningless entertainment, but chose to read books, accumulate knowledge, and cultivate his comprehensive ability. He knew that personal growth could not rely on blind luck, but required persistent effort and rational thinking.

【本段单词】：
1. sociology /ˌsəʊsiˈɒlədʒi/ n. 社会学
2. enable /ɪˈneɪbl/ v. 使能够
3. perspective /pəˈspektɪv/ n. 观点，视角
4. standpoint /ˈstændpɔɪnt/ n. 立场，观点
5. underlying /ˌʌndəˈlaɪɪŋ/ adj. 潜在的，含蓄的
6. idle /ˈaɪdl/ v. 虚度；无所事事 adj. 闲散的
7. cultivate /ˈkʌltɪveɪt/ v. 培养；耕作
8. comprehensive /ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪv/ adj. 综合的，全面的
9. rational /ˈræʃnəl/ adj. 理性的

【段落翻译】：利亚姆的专业是社会学，这门学科让他能够以独特的视角观察人类行为与社会规律。他秉持着一种观点：每一种社会现象都有潜藏的深层原因，没有任何事件是凭空发生的。闲暇之余，他不会把时间虚度在无意义的娱乐上，而是坚持读书、积累学识，培养自身的综合能力。他明白，个人的成长不能依靠侥幸，必须依托持久的努力与理性的思考。

Last semester, the school launched a series of innovative activities to enrich campus life and enhance students’ comprehensive quality. The student council released a formal schedule of various events, including speech contests, volunteer programs, and creative design competitions. At first, Liam was indifferent to these activities, thinking they were time-consuming and had little connection with his academic study. He preferred to stay in the library, focusing on professional reading and data analysis to finish his research report.

【本段单词】：
1. semester /sɪˈmestə(r)/ n. 学期
2. innovative /ˈɪnəveɪtɪv/ adj. 创新的
3. campus /ˈkæmpəs/ n. 校园
4. enhance /ɪnˈhɑːns/ v. 提升，增强
5. council /ˈkaʊnsl/ n. 委员会，理事会
6. formal /ˈfɔːml/ adj. 正式的
7. schedule /ˈʃedjuːl/ n. 时刻表，日程表 v. 安排
8. volunteer /ˌvɒlənˈtɪə(r)/ n. 志愿者 adj. 志愿的 v. 自愿
9. indifferent /ɪnˈdɪfrənt/ adj. 冷漠的；一般的
10. consume /kənˈsjuːm/ v. 消耗，耗费（time-consuming 耗时的）
11. data /ˈdeɪtə/ n. 数据，资料

【段落翻译】：上个学期，学校开展了一系列创新活动，旨在丰富校园生活、提升学生的综合素质。学生会发布了各类活动的正式日程安排，包含演讲比赛、志愿项目以及创意设计大赛。起初，利亚姆对这些活动十分淡然，认为它们耗费时间，和自己的专业学习关联不大。他更愿意待在图书馆，专注专业阅读与数据分析，完成自己的研究报告。

One afternoon, his senior classmate Mabel came to him with a sincere invitation. Mabel was a diligent and optimistic girl who was in charge of the school volunteer agency. She told Liam that the school would launch a public welfare project in the remote suburban community, aiming to deliver cultural resources and daily supplies to elderly residents and left-behind children. The project required patient and responsible volunteers, and Mabel believed Liam was the most appropriate candidate.

【本段单词】：
1. senior /ˈsiːniə(r)/ adj. 年长的；资格较老的 n. 学长，学姐
2. optimistic /ˌɒptɪˈmɪstɪk/ adj. 乐观的
3. agency /ˈeɪdʒənsi/ n. 代理机构；专业部门
4. launch /lɔːntʃ/ v. 发起，启动；发射
5. remote /rɪˈməʊt/ adj. 偏远的；疏远的
6. suburban /səˈbɜːbən/ adj. 郊区的
7. deliver /dɪˈlɪvə(r)/ v. 投递，输送；发表（delivery n. 投递，输送）
8. resident /ˈrezɪdənt/ n. 居民 adj. 居住的
9. responsible /rɪˈspɒnsəbl/ adj. 有责任的；可靠的
10. appropriate /əˈprəʊpriət/ adj. 合适的，恰当的
11. candidate /ˈkændɪdət/ n. 候选人；考生

【段落翻译】：某天下午，他的学姐梅布尔真诚地向他发出了邀请。梅布尔勤勉乐观，负责学校的志愿机构工作。她告诉利亚姆，学校即将在偏远的郊区社区开展一项公益项目，为老年居民和留守儿童输送文化资源与日常物资。该项目需要耐心、有责任心的志愿者，梅布尔认为利亚姆是最合适的人选。

Liam hesitated for a while. On the one hand, he had a tight routine of study and worried that the project would disturb his revision plan. On the other hand, he knew that social practice could broaden his horizon and make up for the limitation of book knowledge. After a brief hesitation, he finally made a definite resolution to join the team. He thought that true growth never came from rigid study alone, but from diverse life experiences and sincere communication with others.

【本段单词】：
1. routine /ruːˈtiːn/ n. 日常惯例 adj. 例行的
2. revision /rɪˈvɪʒn/ n. 复习；修订（revise v. 复习，修订）
3. limitation /ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/ n. 局限，限制
4. brief /briːf/ adj. 简短的，短暂的
5. definite /ˈdefɪnət/ adj. 明确的，一定的（definitely adv. 明确地）
6. resolution /ˌrezəˈluːʃn/ n. 决心；决议；解决
7. rigid /ˈrɪdʒɪd/ adj. 死板的，僵硬的
8. diverse /daɪˈvɜːs/ adj. 多样的，不同的

【段落翻译】：利亚姆犹豫了片刻。一方面，他的学习作息十分紧凑，担心项目会打乱自己的复习计划；另一方面，他清楚社会实践能够开阔眼界，弥补书本知识的局限性。短暂犹豫后，他最终下定决心加入志愿团队。他认为，真正的成长从不只源于刻板的书本学习，更来自丰富多样的人生阅历和真诚的人际沟通。

The volunteer team had a strict but humane operation mechanism. Before the official start, the team leader held a training session to define every member’s duty and clarify the overall procedure. He emphasized that all volunteers must abide by unified rules, prohibit arbitrary behavior, and prioritize the needs of the community residents. Any careless mistake would possibly disturb the progress of the whole project and cause unnecessary trouble and nuisance.

【本段单词】：
1. humane /hjuːˈmeɪn/ adj. 人道的，仁慈的
2. mechanism /ˈmekənɪzəm/ n. 机制，体制
3. define /dɪˈfaɪn/ v. 界定；下定义
4. prohibit /prəˈhɪbɪt/ v. 禁止，阻止
5. arbitrary /ˈɑːbɪtrəri/ adj. 随意的，武断的
6. prioritize /praɪˈɒrətaɪz/ v. 优先考虑
7. nuisance /ˈnjuːsns/ n. 令人讨厌的人/事物；麻烦事

【段落翻译】：这支志愿团队有着严格却人性化的运行机制。正式开展工作前，队长组织了一场培训会，明确了每位成员的职责，梳理了整体工作流程。他强调，所有志愿者必须遵守统一规则，杜绝随意妄为，优先考虑社区居民的需求。任何粗心的失误，都可能打乱整个项目的进度，造成不必要的麻烦与困扰。

On the day of departure, the weather was superb, with soft sunshine and gentle breeze. The team gathered at the school gate, carrying various supplies and cultural appliances. The journey to the suburban community was smooth, with green fields stretching along the road and distant hills standing in vertical layers. Liam caught a glimpse of rural scenery different from the urban bustle, and his tense mood gradually relaxed.

【本段单词】：
1. superb /suːˈpɜːb/ adj. 极好的，绝佳的
2. appliance /əˈplaɪəns/ n. 器具，器械
3. vertical /ˈvɜːtɪkl/ adj. 垂直的
4. glimpse /ɡlɪmps/ n. 一瞥 v. 瞥见
5. bustle /ˈbʌsl/ n. 喧嚣，忙碌
6. tension /ˈtenʃn/ n. 紧张，紧绷（tense adj. 紧张的）

【段落翻译】：出发当天，天气格外晴朗，阳光柔和、微风习习。团队成员在校门口集合，携带各类物资和文化用具。前往郊区社区的路途十分顺畅，沿路绿野绵延，远山层层叠叠、垂直错落。利亚姆瞥见了不同于城市喧嚣的乡村景致，紧绷的心情渐渐舒缓下来。

When they arrived, they found the community environment was far better than they had presumed. The local residents were simple and warm, with no nasty prejudice against outsiders. Many elderly people sat in the courtyard, chatting leisurely, while some children played and scratched and chased each other on the open ground. The overall atmosphere was harmonious and peaceful, without any fierce conflict or violent quarrel.

【本段单词】：
1. presume /prɪˈzjuːm/ v. 推测，假定（presumably adv. 大概，想必）
2. nasty /ˈnɑːsti/ adj. 令人不快的；恶劣的
3. prejudice /ˈpredʒədɪs/ n. 偏见，成见
4. scratch /skrætʃ/ v. 抓，刮擦 n. 抓痕
5. fierce /fɪəs/ adj. 激烈的；凶猛的
6. violent /ˈvaɪələnt/ adj. 暴力的；猛烈的
7. conflict /ˈkɒnflɪkt/ n. 冲突，矛盾

【段落翻译】：抵达目的地后，他们发现社区环境远比预想中更好。当地居民淳朴热忱，对外来者没有恶劣的偏见。许多老人坐在院子里悠闲闲谈，孩子们在空地上嬉笑打闹、追逐奔跑。整体氛围和谐安宁，没有激烈的矛盾冲突，也没有暴躁的争吵。

The team was divided into several segments to carry out different tasks. Some members were responsible for tutoring left-behind children, some for delivering daily necessities, and others for organizing cultural activities for the elderly. Liam was assigned to sort out donated books and supplement the community’s rural library. He needed to classify different types of books, stick clear labels, and make a detailed diagram for book management.

【本段单词】：
1. segment /ˈseɡmənt/ n. 部分，片段
2. supplement /ˈsʌplɪmənt/ v. 补充，增补 n. 增补物
3. classify /ˈklæsɪfaɪ/ v. 分类
4. diagram /ˈdaɪəɡræm/ n. 图表，简图

【段落翻译】：团队被分成若干小组，分工执行不同任务。部分成员负责辅导留守儿童，部分负责配送生活用品，还有成员为老人组织文化活动。利亚姆被安排整理捐赠书籍，为社区乡村图书馆补充藏书。他需要对各类书籍进行分类、粘贴清晰标签，并制作详细的图书管理图表。

While working, Liam met an elderly veteran named Mr. Hale. He was a heroic old man who had served in the navy decades ago. Despite his old age, his mind was still clear, and he had profound insights into life and society. He told Liam many precious stories about social changes and personal growth, which greatly absorbed Liam’s attention and broadened his cognition.

【本段单词】：
1. veteran /ˈvetərən/ n. 老兵；老手
2. heroic /həˈrəʊɪk/ adj. 英勇的，英雄的
3. navy /ˈneɪvi/ n. 海军
4. profound /prəˈfaʊnd/ adj. 深刻的
5. absorb /əbˈsɔːb/ v. 吸收；使全神贯注

【段落翻译】：工作期间，利亚姆遇到了一位名叫黑尔的老退伍军人。这位英勇的老人数十年前曾服役于海军。虽年事已高，但他思维依旧清晰，对人生与社会有着深刻的见解。他给利亚姆讲述了许多关于社会变迁与个人成长的珍贵故事，深深吸引了利亚姆，拓宽了他的认知边界。

Mr. Hale said that many young people nowadays lack perseverance and are easy to resent trivial setbacks. Some even dislike hard work and crave for instant success, which is a terrible mindset. He stressed that true maturity comes from conquering difficulties bravely, not escaping from challenges. Every penalty and hardship in life is a precious lesson that shapes one’s character.

【本段单词】：
1. perseverance /ˌpɜːsəˈvɪərəns/ n. 毅力，坚持
2. resent /rɪˈzent/ v. 怨恨，不满
3. dislike /dɪsˈlaɪk/ v./n. 不喜欢，厌恶
4. instant /ˈɪnstənt/ adj. 即刻的；速成的
5. mature /məˈtʃʊə(r)/ adj. 成熟的 v. （使）成熟
6. conquer /ˈkɒŋkə(r)/ v. 征服；克服
7. penalty /ˈpenəlti/ n. 惩罚，处罚；不利后果

【段落翻译】：黑尔先生说，如今很多年轻人缺乏毅力，容易为琐碎的挫折心生不满，有的人甚至厌恶辛苦付出，渴求速成的成功，这是一种极其糟糕的心态。他强调，真正的成熟源于勇敢克服困难，而非逃避挑战。人生中每一次挫折与磨难，都是塑造个人品格的宝贵课程。

Liam was deeply touched by his words. He used to complain about the tedious pressure of study and occasionally felt frustrated when facing failure. He realized that he had been too fragile and always wanted to halt his pace when encountering difficulties. From that moment on, he made up his mind to adjust his attitude and face challenges positively.

【本段单词】：
1. tedious /ˈtiːdiəs/ adj. 乏味的，冗长的
2. frustrate /frʌˈstreɪt/ v. 使沮丧；挫败
3. fragile /ˈfrædʒaɪl/ adj. 脆弱的
4. halt /hɔːlt/ v./n. 停止，暂停
5. attitude /ˈætɪtjuːd/ n. 态度，看法

【段落翻译】：利亚姆被他的话深深触动。从前的他总会抱怨枯燥的学业压力，遭遇失败时也时常心生颓丧。他意识到自己太过脆弱，遇到困境就想止步不前。从那一刻起，他下定决心调整心态，积极直面所有挑战。

In the afternoon, the team organized a small cultural performance. Some children sang songs, some performed sketches, and the elderly shared their life experiences. The whole scene was warm and lively. No one felt lonely or marginalized. The sincere interaction between people eliminated the estrangement between urban and rural groups and stirred warm emotions in everyone’s heart.

【本段单词】：
1. sketch /sketʃ/ n. 小品；素描；梗概
2. marginalize /ˈmɑːdʒɪnəlaɪz/ v. 边缘化
3. eliminate /ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt/ v. 消除，清除
4. estrangement /ɪˈstreɪndʒmənt/ n. 隔阂，疏远
5. stir /stɜː(r)/ v. 搅动；打动，激起

【段落翻译】：下午，团队组织了一场小型文艺汇演。孩子们放声歌唱、表演小品，老人们分享自己的人生经历，整场活动温暖热闹，没有人感到孤独或被冷落。人与人之间真诚的互动，消解了城乡群体之间的隔阂，在每个人心中激荡起温暖的情愫。

During the activity, Liam found a little girl who was quiet and shy, always staying alone in the corner. He took the initiative to talk with her and tried to squeeze out time to accompany her. Gradually, the girl opened up and began to share her little wishes and trivial troubles. Liam learned that she longed for new books and hoped to learn more knowledge to change her future fate.

【本段单词】：
1. squeeze /skwiːz/ v. 挤出（时间）；挤压
2. trivial /ˈtrɪviəl/ adj. 琐碎的，微小的

【段落翻译】：活动过程中，利亚姆发现一个安静腼腆的小女孩，总是独自待在角落。他主动上前和她聊天，尽力挤出时间陪伴她。渐渐地，小女孩敞开心扉，开始和他分享自己小小的心愿与琐碎的烦恼。利亚姆了解到，她一直渴望拥有新书，希望通过学习更多知识改变未来的命运。

As the day drew to a close, all volunteers gathered to summarize the day’s work. Everyone spoke freely, exchanging their gains and feelings. Some people mentioned the improvement of their communication skills, others talked about the deeper understanding of public welfare. Furthermore, everyone reached a consensus that trivial kindness could gather into great power and warm the whole society.

【本段单词】：
1. furthermore /ˈfɜːðəmɔː(r)/ adv. 此外，而且
2. consensus /kənˈsensəs/ n. 共识，一致同意

【段落翻译】：天色渐晚，所有志愿者聚集在一起总结当日工作。大家畅所欲言，交流自己的收获与感悟。有人提到自己的沟通能力得到了提升，有人谈及对公益事业有了更深刻的认知。除此之外，所有人达成了共识：微小的善意汇聚起来，便能凝聚成巨大的力量，温暖整个社会。

On the way back to school, Liam watched the sunset melt into the distant sky. The cold chill of the evening faded away, replaced by warm peace in his heart. He no longer regarded social practice as a meaningless burden, but realized that every sincere dedication could bring unique value and profound growth.

【本段单词】：
1. melt /melt/ v. 融化；消散
2. chill /tʃɪl/ n. 寒意，凉意 v. 使变冷
3. dedication /ˌdedɪˈkeɪʃn/ n. 奉献，投入

【段落翻译】：返校途中，利亚姆看着落日消融在远方的天际。夜晚的寒凉渐渐褪去，心底只剩下温暖与平和。他不再将社会实践视作毫无意义的负担，而是明白每一份真诚的付出，都能创造独特的价值，带来深刻的成长。

This volunteer experience became a crucial turning point in Liam’s college life. He completely altered his previous indifferent attitude towards social activities and began to actively participate in various public welfare projects. He also adjusted his study pace, balanced academic tasks and social practice, and made his college life more substantial and meaningful.

【本段单词】：
